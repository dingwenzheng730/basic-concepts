1. What is OOP A model that organizes software design around data & objects rather than logic & functions. 2. What is object? a data field that has unique attributes and behaviours. 3. What are the 4 basics of OOP? i) Encapsulation: wrapping up of data under a single unit. ii) Abstraction: hides the internal implementation details. iii) Inheritance: base on class upon another class. iv) Polymorphism: ability of an object to take many forms.
